Rhodes Island

The main organization and the hope for the infected.

Rhodes Island, made up almost exclusively of the Infected legally operates as a pharmaceutical company that provides care and treatment to the Infected, but due to the wide variety of Operators in its employ with combat skills, they are more of a paramilitary organization who strives to fight for the betterment of the Infected. The leader, Amiya, will always be a bright in the darkness.

Penguin Logistic

A cheerful organization.

It is a global logistics and delivery service company run by the famous rapper Emperor. The company is small in size, but is frequently involved in altercations with gangsters and even the Siracusan mafiosi, leading to questions surrounding its legality.

Lungmen

The lungmen guard it´s the most powerful in Terra.

Lungmen's treatment towards the Infected is known to be harsh, though not to the same degree as the neighboring Ursus; prior to Reunion's invasion, the majority of the Infected Lungmenites were confined into slums where they live in lower standards than the rest of the Lungmenites. The situation has improved since, however, with the slums now developed and integrated to Lungmen's downtown.

Laterano

The Sanktas have a halo and wings.

Laterano is notably the leading manufacturer of firearms that holds a monopoly over the industry in Terra. Laterano is also the only country with liberal gun laws in which all Laterans are mandated to own a "patron firearm" for self-defense, whether they are military personnel, law enforcement officers or civilians.

Kazimierz

Mostly the people in Kazimierz are Knights.

Kazimierz is famed for its knightly traditions for generations. However, ever since the rise of Kazimierz's capitalists, such tradition has been gradually corrupted by greed, leading to the heavily commercialization of its triennial Kazimierz Major. While the bustling capitalism bolsters Kazimierz's economy and improves the citizens' livelihood, it also causes several social issues such as inequality.

Victoria

Recently, in this region there are a lot of civil wars.

As a hegemonic and powerful empire, the Victorian military utilizes advanced weaponry, ranging from handheld cannons and piledriver spears to gigantic howitzers and even steam-powered armor. In terms of soft power, Victoria is also known for its ideals of liberty, romanticism and its rich culture. In present day, Victoria retains its influence across Terra, although internal division caused by a series of feuds between royal houses and strong competition from other rising industrial powers such as Kazimierz and Columbia are threatening to topple its position.
